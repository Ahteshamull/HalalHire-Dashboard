HALALHIRE PROJECT FLOW
===================================

1. SYSTEM ARCHITECTURE
-----------------------------------
- Frontend (Next.js): Job Seekers & Companies interface.
- Dashboard (Next.js): Administrator panel.
- Backend (Node.js/Express): Central REST API & Socket.io server.
- Database: MongoDB.

2. USER JOURNEY FLOW (FRONTEND)
-----------------------------------
Step 1: Registration & Onboarding
- A Visitor lands on the Home Page and clicks 'Sign Up'.
- They choose a role: 'Job Seeker' (Individual) or 'Company'.
- The user fills out their respective profiles (verifying identity, uploading documents, etc.).

Step 2: Job Creation (Companies)
- A verified Company logs in and navigates to 'Post a Job'.
- They enter details (including specific Islamic workplace ethics like halal food or prayer room access).
- The job is published to the public '/jobs' board.

Step 3: Job Application (Job Seekers)
- A Job Seeker searches the '/jobs' board.
- They find a suitable job and click 'Apply'.
- The Backend processes the application and triggers a real-time Socket.io notification to the Company.

3. ADMIN MANAGEMENT FLOW (DASHBOARD)
-----------------------------------
Step 1: Verification & Monitoring
- An Admin logs into the Dashboard.
- The Admin reviews new Company registrations.
- If the company meets Islamic ethical standards, the Admin grants them the "Halal Verified" badge.
- Admins can block or manage abusive users from the Users Table.

Step 2: Support & Contact
- A user on the Frontend submits a 'Contact Us' ticket.
- The Admin receives a notification in the Dashboard.
- The Admin reviews the message in the Contact Table, resolves the issue, and deletes the ticket.

Step 3: Subscriptions
- Companies purchase premium subscription plans via the Frontend to post more jobs.
- The Admin tracks active plans, revenue, and billing histories via the Subscriptions tab.
